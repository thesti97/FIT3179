World Airports Overview

This visualisation provide an overview of the airport density in the world.

A map overview of the allocation of airports in the world

The map shows the lcoation for every single airport in the world. We can get a clear view on how the airport is scattered around our world. The biggest airport hotspot are the US and Europe. The second hotspot is in South America, mainly in Brazil. The third tier locations are in the south part of Africa, Korea, Japan and Oceania.

Airport number by country

The dot chart shows us the number of airports each country has, from this visualisation we can tell from first glance that most country in the world has a small number of airports as the density of dots is the highest in the bottom-left corner of the chart. Only a few countries has an airport number over 300 and a handful of countries has more than 1000 airports. This is possibly due to the geographical reason and countries development level.

Paved airports per country

The previous visualisations include all the airport each country has: finished, unpaved and planned. However, this chart only includes the paved, or finished airports.
